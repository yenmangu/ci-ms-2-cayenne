Cayenne Features

Explore how Cayenne helps you cook smarter, faster, and with more flavour.

Search by Ingredient

Cayenne allows you to search recipes based on ingredients you already have at home. Just enter what’s in your fridge or pantry and discover delicious recipes that reduce food waste and inspire creative cooking.

Recipe Details & Features

The recipe detail page gives you everything you need to cook with confidence. Here you'll find a full breakdown for each dish, including a photo, clear instructions, and ingredients.

  • Step-by-step Instructions: Easy-to-follow cooking steps, with ingredient amounts shown in your preferred units (metric or US). Switch units at any time using the toggle in the app header—amounts and labels update instantly.
  • Smart Ingredient List: Each ingredient is shown as a mini card, with an image, amount, and unit. Tap the basket icon to add any item directly to your shopping list
  • Save Your Favourites: Like what you see? Tap the heart icon to add the recipe to your personal favourites. Your saved recipes are stored locally and always available.
  • Mobile-Friendly Design: All detail pages are designed mobile-first, so you can check recipes in the kitchen or on the go.

Need to come back to a recipe? All your saved favourites can be accessed anytime via the favourites tab in the header.

Save Your Favourites

Found something you love? Tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your personal collection. Favourites are stored in your browser and persist between visits - no login required.

Access all your saved dishes from the favourites tab. Perfect for meal planning or revisiting tried-and-true meals.

Build a Shopping List

Add recipe ingredients directly to a dynamic shopping list. Cayenne automatically groups duplicates and lets you tick off items as you shop.

The shopping list is stored locally, so you can build your meal plan throughout the week and head to the shop prepared.
