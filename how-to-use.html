How to Use Cayenne

A quick guide to finding recipes, saving favourites, and building a shopping list.

Navigation tip: Cayenne uses hash-based navigation. Please use the links and buttons inside the app to move around. Avoid the browser's Back/Forward buttons to prevent losing your place.

Getting Started

  1. Open the app and locate the search bar on the home page.
  2. Type one or more ingredients you already have (e.g., “chicken, basil, tomato”).
  3. Press Search to see matching recipes.

Results are tailored to your ingredients so you can cook what you already own and reduce food waste.

Understanding Your Results

  • Recipe cards show a photo, title, and a quick overview. Select any card to view the full recipe.
  • Like (♥) a recipe to save it to your Favourites for easy access later.
  • The grid is mobile-first and scrollable. Tap a card to continue to details.

You can return to the results at any time by using the in-app navigation (e.g., the header links), rather than the browser Back button.

Recipe Details

The detail view gives you everything you need to cook with confidence: a large photo, ingredients, and clear instructions.

  • Units toggle: Switch between metric and US units in the header. Amounts and labels update instantly.
  • Ingredient mini-cards: Each item shows its name, amount, and unit at a glance. Add items to your shopping list with a single tap.
  • Step-by-step method: Easy to follow on phones and tablets while you cook.

When you're done, use the header to navigate elsewhere in the app. This keeps your place safe.

Save Your Favourites

Found something you love? Tap the heart icon to save a recipe. Your favourites are stored in your browser and persist between visits—no account needed.

  • Open the Favourites tab in the header to view your saved recipes.
  • Un-favourite at any time to remove it from the list.

Build a Shopping List

Add ingredients from any recipe to your shopping list. Cayenne groups duplicates to keep it tidy, and lets you tick items off as you shop.

  • Open Shopping List from the header to see everything you've added.
  • Your list is stored locally, so it's available on your next visit.

Tips for a Smooth Experience

  • Use the header links to move between Home, Favourites, and Shopping List.
  • Prefer the in-app navigation over the browser Back/Forward buttons.
  • If an image takes a moment to load, a placeholder appears so you can keep reading.
  • Switch units at any time—amounts update instantly in the detail view.

Accessibility

  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and controls throughout.
  • Meaningful alt text for images and clear labelling on interactive elements.
  • Mobile-first layout with responsive text and touch-friendly targets.

Troubleshooting

I clicked a link and landed on the wrong page

Use the header links to navigate back to the section you need. Avoid the browser's Back/Forward buttons while using Cayenne.

My favourites or list look empty

If you've recently cleared your browser data or switched device, your saved items may not appear. Save or add items again—they'll persist on your current device.
