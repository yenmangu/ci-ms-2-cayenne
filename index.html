About the App

Cayenne is a modern recipe discovery app and website designed for cooks of all skill levels. Whether you're looking for new meal inspiration, want to experiment with global cuisines, or need to find recipes based on ingredients you already have, Cayenne streamlines the search process with an easy-to-use, mobile-first interface. The app draws on the Spoonacular API to provide a huge, regularly updated collection of recipes, all filtered and displayed in an intuitive, visually rich format.

Features
Search by Ingredient

Cayenne's ingredient search lets you quickly find recipes based on what you already have at home. Simply enter one or more ingredients, such as “chicken”, “broccoli”, or “rice”, and Cayenne will suggest a range of recipes that use those items. This feature is ideal for reducing food waste, planning meals around what's in your fridge, and discovering new ideas without extra trips to the shop. Results appear quickly, are relevant to your needs, and can be filtered further by cuisine or dietary preference, making meal planning both efficient and inspiring.
Save Your Favourites

With Cayenne, you can easily save your favourite recipes for quick access later. Whenever you find a dish you like, simply click the heart icon to add it to your personal favourites list. These saved recipes are stored securely in your browser, so you can revisit them any time without needing an account or signing in. Whether you're meal planning for the week or building a collection of go-to dishes, the favourites feature keeps all your top picks just a tap away.
